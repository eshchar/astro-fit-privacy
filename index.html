Astro Fit — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Astro Fit is a single-player arcade game for Android. This policy explains what information the app handles. The short version: we do not collect, store, or sell any personal data ourselves. The only data collection in the app comes from Google's advertising SDK, described below.

Data we collect

None. Astro Fit has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics of our own. Your scores and settings are saved only on your device and are never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app deletes them.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Astro Fit shows one advertisement at most, only when the app is opened — never during gameplay. Ads are served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google's SDK may collect device information such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, ad interaction data, and diagnostic information. This collection is governed by Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data in advertising

You can reset or delete your advertising ID, or opt out of ad personalization, in your device's settings under Settings → Privacy → Ads.

Feedback you send us

The in-game "Send Feedback" option opens your own email app with a pre-filled message. Sending it is entirely voluntary. If you send feedback we will receive your email address and whatever you wrote, and we use them only to read and respond to your message.

Children

Astro Fit is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy (for example, if a future update adds online leaderboards), we will update this page and the effective date above.
